Site Name: Clean Energy Nigeria

A platform to provide information about eco-friendly energy solutions in Nigeria and also create awareness about the dangers of climate change and global warming.

Site's Purpose

The site provides a medium to raise awareness about renewable energy sources in Nigeria and their benefits, promoting Sustainable practices and encouraging individuals and businesses to adopt clean energy solutions.

Scenarios

  • What is clean energy, and why is it important?
  • How does solar/wind/hydro energy work?
  • What are the benefits of switching to renewable energy?
  • What impact does clean energy have on the environment?

Typography

  • Roboto for Headiings
  • San-serif for body and paragraphs

Color Schema

  • Primary Color: Green (#0b6623) for the navbar and footer.
  • Secondary Color: Sky Blue (#17a2b8) for sections.
  • Accent Color: Solar Gold (#ffc.107)
  • Background Color: Soft White (#f8f9fa) for body background
  • Text Color: Charcoal Grey (#343a40)
