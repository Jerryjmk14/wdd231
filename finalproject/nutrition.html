5. Improves Mental Focus: A good mix of nutrients helps you stay sharp‚Äîgreat for students, workers, and elders alike.


üçõ What Should Be on a Plate?


1. Half your plate: Vegetables and fruits like okra, ugu, carrots, garden eggs, and mangoes.

2. One-quarter: Whole grains or starchy foods like brown rice, amala, fufu, or ofada rice.

3. One-quarter: Lean proteins like beans, moi moi, grilled fish, or chicken.

4. Plus: A small portion of healthy fats (groundnuts, avocado, or palm oil) and plenty of water.
